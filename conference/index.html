K.C Youth Summit

15 March, 2019

K.C College, Churchgate, Mumbai - 400020

About K.C Youth Summit

Kc youth summit is a one-day conference enhancing the intellect and imbibing global awareness of the youth. A quintessential conference in its own. The summit aims to provide opportunity to the students to put forwards their views about domestic and international issues. At KCYS great ideas are conferred which facilitate the youth to undertake decisions which benefits the society.

About Our Theme

"Unfolding The Truth"

In the era of ambiguity and opaqueness, we at KC aim to help the students revalidate their own thoughts, unfolding the true nature of events. We focus to remove the stigma of reluctance and thrive to speak the unspoken. Our aim is to enlighten the youth and foster development of their opinions through healthy discussions about the issues prevalent in the domestic and international economy. We endeavour to inculcate social values and principles of personal excellence.

About K.C College

Kishinchand Chellaram College is one of the premier institutions affiliated to the University of Mumbai and recognized by Government of Maharashtra. It was established in 1954 by the Hyderabad (Sind) National Collegiate Board. Over the years K.C. has established itself as one of the leading colleges in Mumbai. This fact is well supported by excellent results, both at HSC as well as at the University examinations and the number of laurels won by the students. The Motto of the College is ‘SALVATION THROUGH KNOWLEDGE’. This motto was selected from ancient Sanskrit scriptures. ‘Knowledge’ in the highest sense means acquiring wisdom & understanding, which in turn leads man to” Moksha” or Salvation. K.C. prides itself in motivating students to achieve excellence not only in academic fields but also in co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

From Principal's Desk

Dr.(Mrs.)Hemlata K Bagla

Youth is the golden resource of our nation. Today's youth is brimming with potential and unexplored opportunities. The youth needs to understand their role and the responsibilities in making of the country. With a view of moulding and sharpening the pool of opportunities of our country's Youth, we have come up with this novel venture of KC Youth Summit. Addressing burning issues, like water crisis, political awareness, media influence. We aim to open doors of various perspectives and exemplify the spark of curiosity and eagerness in students. Since a majority of our country's population is in the productive stage, this initiative would throw light on a plethora of social issues and develop a more enhanced youth. Enlightened by the crème de la crème of today's social circuit.I would like to express my best wishes to the committee member and people who ideated the event.

CSR

The recent spine chilling terrorist attack at Pulwama has shook us from the depths of our being. In our apathetic and conflict driven times, it is the commitment and actions of young people that revitalise the nation. We, the students of K.C College believe that conflict in itself does not render dialogue impossible, it is our unwillingness. This is why it is crucial to be the one to initiate dialogue. We chose to respond by organising a Silent March as a pledge to our young soldiers to realise peace. K.C Youth Summit is the seed that we are sowing today which will blossom into a magnificent future where no one is left behind !
